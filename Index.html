Brief Guide to the Medicine Buddha Practice

简明药师佛修持

Medicine Buddha practice is not only something we do on a cushion. It is also how we care for the body, speech, and mind in daily life.

When we choose healthy food instead of harmful food, that is Medicine Buddha practice.

When we exercise, rest properly, and avoid habits that damage the body, that is Medicine Buddha practice.

When we think positively, speak gently, and calm the mind with meditation, that is also Medicine Buddha practice.

Medicine Buddha teaches us that healing is not only prayer. Healing is wise action. Every good decision becomes part of the path.

Sit quietly for a few minutes. Visualize Medicine Buddha in front of you, blue like lapis lazuli, shining with healing light. Imagine that blue light entering your body, calming fear, purifying sickness, and giving strength to make healthy choices.

Then recite the mantra:

✦ The Sacred Mantra ✦
Tayatha Om Bekandze Bekandze
Maha Bekandze Radza Samudgate Soha
喋雅他 嗡 贝堪则 贝堪则
摩诃贝堪则 拉扎萨目嘎喋 梭哈

Recite it 7, 21, or 108 times.

After the mantra, make a simple promise:

May I care for this body wisely.
May I avoid what harms me.
May I eat, move, rest, and live with awareness.
May every healthy habit become Medicine Buddha practice.
May all beings be healed and free from suffering.

Medicine Buddha practice means taking medicine when needed, following good medical advice, eating wisely, exercising regularly, resting well, thinking positively, and using mantra and meditation to support the whole person.

This is healing through body, speech, and mind.

☸

药师佛的修持，不仅仅是坐在禅垫上的功课。它也是我们在日常生活中照顾身、语、意的方式。

当我们选择有益健康的食物，而不选择伤害身体的食物时，这就是药师佛的修持。

当我们适度运动、好好休息，并远离损害身心的不良习惯时，这也是药师佛的修持。

当我们以正念观照自己的念头，以柔和的言语对待他人，并通过禅修安定内心时，这同样是药师佛的修持。

药师佛修持提醒我们：疗愈不只是祈祷。疗愈也是智慧的行动。每一个善巧、健康、清明的选择，都可以成为修行之道的一部分。

安静地坐几分钟。观想药师佛在你面前，身如琉璃蓝，放射出清凉、慈悲、疗愈的光芒。想象这道蓝色光明进入你的身体，平息恐惧，净化疾病，安定身心，并给予你做出健康选择的力量。

然后念诵药师佛心咒，可念诵 7遍、21遍或108遍。

念诵完毕后，发一个简单的善愿：

愿我以智慧照顾此身。
愿我远离一切伤害身心的因缘。
愿我的饮食、运动、休息与生活，都具足正念。
愿每一个健康的习惯，都成为药师佛修持的一部分。
愿一切众生获得疗愈，远离痛苦，安住安乐。

药师佛的修持意味着：需要时服药，遵从专业医疗建议，明智饮食，规律运动，充分休息，培养正向而清明的心态，并以咒语和禅修来支持身心的整体健康。

这就是通过身、语、意进行的疗愈。

☸
