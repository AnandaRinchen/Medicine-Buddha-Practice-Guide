# 🔵 Medicine Buddha Practice Guide
### *Brief Guide to the Medicine Buddha Practice*
### 简明药师佛修持指引

> **Authored by Khenpo Kunga Rinchen**

---

## 📖 About This Project

This repository contains a beautifully designed **bilingual (English & Chinese)** guide to the **Medicine Buddha** (*Sangye Menla*) practice. The guide was authored by **Khenpo Kunga Rinchen** and is presented as a downloadable HTML document with elegant traditional styling.

Medicine Buddha practice is not only something we do on a cushion — it is also how we care for the **body, speech, and mind** in daily life.

---

## ✨ What's Inside

The guide covers six core areas of practice:

| Section | English | Chinese |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | What Is Medicine Buddha Practice? | 何为药师佛修持？ |
| 2 | The Three Aspects of Healing | 疗愈的三个层面 |
| 3 | Visualization Practice | 观想修持 |
| 4 | The Sacred Mantra | 神圣咒语 |
| 5 | The Dedication of Merit | 回向发愿 |
| 6 | Daily Healing Practices | 日常疗愈修行 |

## 🕉️ The Mantra

```
Tayatha Om Bekandze Bekandze
Maha Bekandze Radza Samudgate Soha

喋雅他 嗡 贝堪则 贝堪则
摩诃贝堪则 拉扎萨目嘎喋 梭哈
```

*Recite 7, 21, or 108 times.*

---

## 🙏 The Dedication

> May I care for this body wisely.  
> May I avoid what harms me.  
> May I eat, move, rest, and live with awareness.  
> May every healthy habit become Medicine Buddha practice.  
> May all beings be healed and free from suffering.

> 愿我以智慧照顾此身。  
> 愿我远离一切伤害身心的因缘。  
> 愿我的饮食、运动、休息与生活，都具足正念。  
> 愿每一个健康的习惯，都成为药师佛修持的一部分。  
> 愿一切众生获得疗愈，远离痛苦，安住安乐。
